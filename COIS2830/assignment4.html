Assignment 4

For Assignment 4, I have chosen to do a personal page in the style similar to Matthew Farley's website. I have taken his general website structure, and added the navigation bar at the top. However, the CSS file was written from the scratch. I have also taken a font from Google Fonts, and incorporated it in the website. The logo and main page design were done in Figma, logos for other buttons (including the social media) are taken from Icons8. Some of the code snippets (like the form for Contact Me, Login and Register) were taken from W3Schools.
I have already optimized it for mobile and desktop, and I use responsive column design. As you can see in CSS, I have two set of instructions: one for mobile (screen less than 768px in width) and desktop. I use div tags to separate and align content on pages, and "width: %" property to make parts of page responsive.
I have created a favicon for my website for multiple platforms here.
For Chrome and Safari, I have included a custom scrollbar. It was also taken from W3Schools.
